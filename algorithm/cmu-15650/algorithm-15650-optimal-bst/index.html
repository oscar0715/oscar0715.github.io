总Cost

$ \begin{align*} \text{Expected Cost of tree } & = \sum_{i=1}^n \text{cost}(k_i)p_i \\ & = \sum_{i=1}^n (Depth(T,k_i) + 1) p_i \\ & = \sum_{i=1}^n Depth(T,k_i) p_i + \sum_{i=1}^n p_i \\ & = \left(\sum_{i=1}^n Depth(T,k_i) p_i\right) + 1 \end{align*} $

Solution

Let D(T,k)=Depth(T,k)D(T,k) = Depth(T,k)D(T,k)=Depth(T,k) for brevity.
Let TTT be an optimal tree that has root krk_rk​r​​ .

$$ \begin{eqnarray} C(T) &=& p_r + \sum_{a=1}^{r-1} p_a(D(T_l, K_a) + 1) + \sum_{a=r+1}^{n} p_a(D(T_r, K_a) + 1) \\ &=& p_r + \sum_{a=1}^{r-1} p_a + \sum_{a=1}^{r-1} p_a(D(T_{left}, K_a)) + \sum_{a=r+1}^{n} p_a + \sum_{a=r+1}^{n} p_a(D(T_{right}, K_a)) \\ &=& p_r + \sum_{a=1}^{r-1} p_a + \sum_{a=r+1}^{n} p_a + \sum_{a=1}^{r-1} p_a(D(T_{left}, K_a)) + \sum_{a=r+1}^{n} p_a(D(T_{right}, K_a)) \\ &=& \sum_{a=1}^{n} p_a + C(T_{left}) + C(T_{right}) \end{eqnarray} $$

所以可以用自底向上的方法来求最后树的Cost

Recurrence

C[i,j]C[i,j]C[i,j] := “cost of an optimal binary search tree on keys ki,...,kjk_i,...,k_jk​i​​,...,k​j​​.”

$$ C[i,j] = \left\{ \begin{matrix} 0, & \text{if } j < i \text{ (tree is empty)} \\ p_i, & \text{if } i = j \text{ (tree is single node)}\\ \sum_{a=1}^j P_a + min_r\{ C[i, r-1] + C[r+1, j] \} \end{matrix} \right . $$
