Pacific Atlantic Water Flow

Problem

Example:

Given the following 5x5 matrix:

Pacific ~ ~ ~ ~ ~
~ 1 2 2 3 (5) *
~ 3 2 3 (4) (4) *
~ 2 4 (5) 3 1 *
~ (6) (7) 1 4 5 *
~ (5) 1 1 2 4 *
* * * * * Atlantic

Return:

[[0, 4], [1, 3], [1, 4], [2, 2], [3, 0], [3, 1], [4, 0]] (positions with parentheses in above matrix).

Solution

DFS

  1. 分别用两个数组来判断，每个节点是否能流向 Pacific 或者 Atlantic
